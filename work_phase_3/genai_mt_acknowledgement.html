GenAI MT Acknowledgement

1. How AI Was Used

Generative AI was integrated into several phases of the website design and development process, serving as a versatile assistant across creative, technical, and research tasks:

AI helped create, refine, and debug CSS files. This accelerated the styling process, especially when implementing responsive layouts and cross-browser compatibility.


AI played a critical role in early ideation, helping brainstorm site features and structure. I used it to quickly generate potential sitemap layouts that aligned with user needs and cultural inclusivity goals.


Drafting user-friendly and culturally appropriate content was supported by AI. It helped rewrite and polish text for clarity, tone, and inclusivity across different sections of the site.


AI significantly speed up the process of finding similar websites for design inspiration and feature referencing. Instead of spending hours browsing, I was able to surface relevant examples in minutes.


2. How AI Supported Learning

AI was not only a tool for productivity but also a learning partner throughout the project:

I improved my understanding of CSS structure and responsive design through AI‚Äôs suggestions and explanations during debugging.


By exploring AI-suggested layouts and content flows, I developed a more intuitive sense of how effective information architecture is structured.


With AI providing step-by-step help on issues like layout alignment or navigation behavior, I became more confident in independently solving similar problems later.


AI reduced time spent on preliminary research, allowing me to focus more on deeper design thinking and user testing.


3. Critical Evaluation of AI‚Äôs Effectiveness

While AI was a valuable co-creator, its effectiveness varied depending on the task:

What Worked Well :

AI saved significant time, particularly in generating clean CSS, drafting content, and locating similar websites for reference.


Whether I needed code, copywriting help, or design suggestions, AI was able to provide assistance on demand.


Brainstorming ideas with AI encouraged divergent thinking and brought in perspectives I hadn‚Äôt considered.


Limitations :

AI‚Äôs suggestions sometimes lacked deep context awareness, especially for culturally specific content or user scenarios.


Initial outputs for text or design ideas often required refinement to better fit the project's tone, audience, and goals.


While useful, relying too heavily on AI could hinder deeper learning if not balanced with critical thinking and hands-on exploration.


4. Ethical Considerations

Using AI also raised several ethical considerations that I remained mindful of:

AI-generated content may carry implicit biases or overlook cultural subtleties. I took care to review and adjust outputs to ensure respectful, inclusive representation.


I avoided entering any personal or sensitive information into AI tools, protecting both user privacy and project integrity.


All AI-generated materials were used as starting points, then critically assessed, edited, and integrated to ensure originality and personal contribution to the work.
